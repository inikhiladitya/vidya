title: Arithmetic operators
---

- `+`- Addition
- `-` - Subtraction
- `*` - Multiplication
- `/` - Division
- `%` - Modulo Operation (Remainder after division
- `++` - Increment
- `--` - Decrement
- `+=` - Addition Assignment
- `-=` - Subtraction Assignment
- `*=` - Multiplication Assignment
- `/=` - Divison Assignment
- `%=` - Modulus Assignment
