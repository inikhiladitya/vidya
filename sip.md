- Session Initiation Protocol, operates on [Port 5060](#Port%205060), [Port 5061](#Port%205061)
- Provides signaling and controlling multimedia communication sessions in applications.
- Used for Internet telephony for voice and video calls, [VoIP](#VoIP) and instant messaging.
